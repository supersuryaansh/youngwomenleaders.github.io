Our Story

The Young Women Leaders is social start up founded by a group of friends who started by scribbling their ideas on a piece of paper, Today we try our best to improve the lives of women in our society

TYWL's international, youthful, cutting-edge, and innovative members body is enhanced by the unparalleled opportunity for cross-cultural exploration. Our team members will develop the skills necessary to be comfortable anywhere and effective everywhere. Why not join our fast-growing international women empowering community? Get in touch today to learn more.
